Introducing: Gift Pokémon





Wanna feel like a real Gym Leader?

Greetings Trainer, I am happy to announce you a new feature to the Gym Battles.

How do I get the Gift Pokémon?

You only need to defeat a Stage 2 Gym Leader. After you won the battle I will trade you the Pokémon.
If you allready won in a Stage 2 Gym before this update happend, you'll still get your reward.
